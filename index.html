What is ChandraShila

Chandrashila, Uttarakhand is a popular trekking destination located in the Garhwal region of the Indian state of Uttarakhand. It offers stunning views and a rich blend of natural beauty and spiritual significance.

Location: Garhwal Himalayas, Uttarakhand, India
Elevation: 4,130 meters (13,550 feet)
Meaning: "Moon Rock" in Hindi
Popular trekking destination
Offers stunning 360-degree views of the Himalayas
Sacred Shiva temple at the summit
Moderate trekking route
Best time to visit: Summer (April-June) and Autumn (September-November)
The Trek begins at Chopta, India's "Mini Switzerland"
