03 Days 02 Nights
Best Season Winter (Nov, Dec, Jan) Spring (Feb, March, April)

Who should attend?

The town of Mcleodganj, Himachal Pradesh is famous especially and foremostly for being a spiritual getaway and pilgrimage for the Buddhists. The reason behind that is it is home to the 14th Dalai Lama of the Buddhists, Lama Tenzin Gyatso, who came here in the year of 1960 after his exile from Tibet. Anyone can join us on this perfect getaway to some serene beauty.

Day 0 - DEPARTURE FROM DELHI

Bus volvo
Stay not Applicable
  • Departure in the night.
  • The group will assemble at the pickup point.
  • Overnight journey to Mcleodganj via ac bus.
  • Halt for dinner in between(not on us).

Day 1 - REACH MCLEODGANJ EXPLORE LOCAL MARKET | BHAGSU WATERFALL | CAFE HOPPING

Bus volvo
Stay not Applicable
  • After reaching Mcleodganj, Hotel check-in. Freshen up & rest for some time.
  • After this, go and explore the Mcleodganj flea market.
  • Visit Bhagsu waterfall, Dalai Lama Monastery, and cafes.
  • Back to the hotel.
  • Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2 - TRIUND TOP | START TREKKING FROM BHAGSU | CAMPING UNDER THE STARS

Bus volvo
Stay not Applicable
  • Freshen up and have breakfast.
  • Post this we will proceed for the Triund trek.
  • Triund is the crown jewel of Dharamshala, situated in the laps of the Dhauladhar mountains, it has the perfect view of Dhauladhar mountains on one side and Kangra valley on the other.
  • Reach Triund in the evening, camp check-in.
  • Dinner and overnight stay at the camps.

Day 3 - TREK DOWN | DHARAMSHALA EXPLORATION | DEPARTURE

Bus volvo
Stay not Applicable
  • Wake up early and have your breakfast
  • Start trekking down to Mcleodganj.
  • Reach Mcleodganj, explore flea market then via Dharamshala departure.
  • Reach Triund in the evening, camp check-in.
  • Dinner and overnight stay at the camps.

Day 4 - REACH BACK TO DELHI

Bus volvo
Stay not Applicable

Early morning drop at Delhi.
